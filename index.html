Medical

My name is Joshua Cameron. I spent almost a decade in the medical field. I was honorably discharged after two combat tours and four years in the Army as a combat medic, where I was deployed to, or visited during deployment: Kosovo, Macedonia, Bulgaria, Kuwait, Iraq, Qatar, UAE, Germany and Ireland.

Oil Field

I have vast experience in various different scopes, from the fields in Utah, Wyoming and Colorado, where I have even worked in well below freezing temperatures. I have learned throughout my life that life doesn't wait for you. Life doesn't wait for good weather or calm times.

Life doesn't wait for your convenience. Hard careers sometimes makes for hard lives, but hard choices make for far more experienced people and workers.

Volunteerism

I also have a long history of volunteerism, from 5k runs for charity, security for civilian events while I was a soldier, and now I am a precinct chair for one of the major political parties here in Utah. I have volunteered to speak at many events and rallies, where I try to add energy to the crowd and use my time and skills to add a net positive value to the lives of Utahns, and indirectly, Americans at large.
